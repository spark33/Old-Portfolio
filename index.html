TEF Scheduler

As a scheduling manager for CMU Computing Services, I was asked to manipulate the department’s schedule for student employees on WhenToWork.com. I was absolutely disgusted by the app’s unintuitive interface, lack of flexibility in shift planning and unnecessarily complex user stories; many of them were manually done on other software. The department was also using more than 5 apps to support its functions; any developer would have recognized how easy it would be to support all of them using one app.

Project Introduction

I did not want to work with this app for the next two years. I decided to create a more elegant solution and present it to the department head. I first surveyed current employees to assess issues with WhenToWork and defined focus points for the new app. I then designed the app, a project timeline and MongoDB document structures before onboarding a designer and another developer; neither had previous experience with the MEAN stack.

Code with Music!

Split your team up into two-- the players and the movers.

Players will have to communicate the correct sequence of moves to get the movers to the target by using sounds. For example:

  • 1 clap = Switch Vehicles
  • 2 claps = Turn Left
  • 3 claps = Turn Right
  • 4 claps = Move
  • snap = next move

When you’re ready, tell the TA who the players and movers are. Once you’ve gotten your sheets, no one is allowed to speak!

There will be point penalties if you speak!
